About The Maker

Hi! I am the programmer of this website, and my name is Ricky Kelley. I got an interest in computers when I was just a little kid. I used to play games on the family computer. I messed with the browser and learned my way around the computer. When I was 9 years old, I found Kahn Academy.
Not long after, I found computer coding at Kahn Academy, where I learned basic JS (JavaScript). When I decided I was finished with js, I decided I needed to learn more code.
By the time I finished js, I was 11 years old and ready to learn how to make websites. So, I took the HTML and CSS courses at Kahn Academy, and it paid off! I now make projects for my friends using either js or HTML. I decided to learn code because I thought it would be fun and cool. I was right!
